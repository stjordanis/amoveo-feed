Amoveo Exchange Instructions

For sellers

  1. Go to exchange home
  2. Deposit required VEO to exchange account
  3. Wait for 4 confirmations.
  4. Fill all required field:
    • bitcoin address that will receive bitcoins - Your bitcoin address
    • address that will receive VEO - Buyer's VEO address
    • time limit in seconds. If the bitcoin doesn't arrive within this limit, then the veo is refunded - keep it by default
    • how many veo to sell - Amount of VEO you're selling
    • how many bitcoin to buy - Total amount of BTC, that Buyer will send you.
  5. Click on generate unsigned trade request
  6. Copy unsigned trade request (whole string) and sign it with the light wallet
  7. Copy signed request from wallet and paste in into field next to publish signed trade request and click on this button.
  8. Give received trade request to buyer.
  9. Wait for the payment.

For buyers

  1. Go to main page of the exchange and get trade request from seller.
  2. Put that request into lookup trade field, click on the button and check all details of the deal (very carefully check your VEO address, amount of VEO and BTC)
  3. Send required BTC to seller address.
  4. Wait for 3 confirmation
  5. Withdraw your veo from the exchange
    • put your pubkey into the "lookup account" spot
    • click "generate unsigned request to withdraw funds"
    • Copy unsigned request (whole string) and sign it with light wallet http://159.65.120.84:8080/wallet.html
    • Copy signed request from wallet and paste in into field next to "publish signed request to withdraw funds" and click on this button.
